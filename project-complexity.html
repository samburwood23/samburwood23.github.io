Complexity Theory & AI Agents

This project explores how ideas from complexity theory, information theory, and quantum mechanics shape what AI agents can and cannot do efficiently.

It focuses on the gap between verification (recognising that a solution is correct) and generation (constructing that solution), and what that means for search, creativity, and agent design.
